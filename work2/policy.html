ENS POLICY MESSAGE


ABC Enterprises is one of the leading companies in the waste management sector. Our priority is Environment, Health & Safety by providing & maintaining safe & healthy working conditions and up keeping our processes and operations by raising awareness about EHS amongst employees, contractors & suppliers. The environmental aspects and health and safety hazards associated with our activities, taking action on the identification and ensuring a commitment to prevent pollution from our activities. ABC Enterprises doesn't compromise on safety. The safety of employees involved directly or indirectly is an absolute priority.

Comply with applicable Environmental Laws & Regulations, and set internal requirements to ISO 14001:2015. As appropriate, take corrective actions where past practices have harmed the Environment. The success of this policy will depend on each employee's cooperation and will review on an ongoing basis. Staff and others to whom this policy applies should feel free to put forward suggestions at any time. This policy is based on and complements ABC Enterprises' core values.
